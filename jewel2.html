Why strong candidates still struggle with PhD proposals

  • Expectations are rarely made explicit
  • Good ideas lack structure and focus
  • Proposals don’t align with funding or supervisors
  • Feedback is vague, inconsistent, or contradictory

These challenges affect applicants from all backgrounds — not because they lack ability, but because proposal writing is an acquired skill.

What we do

We provide structured, academically grounded support to help you clarify your research question, frame it appropriately, and present it in a way that supervisors and funders recognise as viable.

How it works

  1. Clarify your idea

    We identify the core research question and scope.

  2. Align with expectations

    We shape the proposal around funding calls and supervisory fit.

  3. Build a coherent structure

    Methods, rationale, and feasibility are made explicit.

  4. Refine and strengthen

    The proposal is reviewed with interview and funding criteria in mind.
