FAQ

Questions you might have

A few of the most common questions from PhD applicants. You can also ask anything specific in your initial message.

Do you write everything for me from scratch?
We can help at different levels — from structuring and editing your own text to drafting sections based on your ideas and materials. You remain the owner of the proposal and responsible for its content.
Is this allowed by universities?
Our role is to support clarity, structure, and communication of your own work and ideas. We do not fabricate data, results, or credentials, and you are responsible for following your institution’s policies.
How much does it cost?
Pricing depends on the length of the proposal, your starting point (idea vs. full draft), and the timeline. Share your details and we’ll provide a clear quote before any work starts.
Which subjects do you work with?
We support a range of fields including life sciences, social sciences, public health, quantitative disciplines, and interdisciplinary projects. If your topic is highly specialised, we’ll let you know if it’s not a fit.
Can you help with interviews as well?
Yes. Once your proposal is in good shape, we can offer mock interview sessions focused on your project, motivation, and basic methods to help you feel more confident.
What happens after I submit the form?
You’ll receive a short follow-up email (or WhatsApp message, if you prefer) to clarify your needs. Then you’ll get a plan, timeline, and quote. You can decide whether to go ahead.
